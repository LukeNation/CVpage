About me

I'm a backend developer currently working at NacionServicios. My main stack is Java + Spring Boot, with experience across core banking systems (T24/Temenos), e-commerce platforms, and large-scale freight management systems. I studied Computer Science at UNLP and AI Engineering at Universidad de Palermo.

I care deeply about clean, efficient code. My interest in object-oriented design is backed by hardware awareness — I've been diagnosing and repairing my own machines for years, which translates directly into writing code that's conscious of resource usage and response times, not just correctness.

I always try to understand both the business and the infrastructure behind what I'm building. That lets me get ahead of requests before they're made, push back when something can be done better, and propose solutions that actually fit the existing system — not just what sounds good on paper.
